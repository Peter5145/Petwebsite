1.The German Shepherd

The German sherpherd or Atlastian is a Greman breed of a working dog of medium to large size.The breed was developed by Max Von Stephanitz using various traditional Gemran herding dogs from 1899. It was originally bred as a herding dog, for herding sheep.

About

-They are known for their intelligence and many can learn a new behaviour in only a few repetitions.

-They have a speed of 48km/h (maximum, running)

-The male weighs 30-40kg, while the female weihgs 22-32kg

2. The Wolf Hybrid

The Wolf Hybrid is a canine produced by the mating of a domestic dog with a gray wolf, eastern wolf, red wolf, or Ethopian wolf to produce a hybrid.

About

-They shy away from their owners, no matter how patient and kind.

-Typical wolfdog characteristics, of varying degrees are: fearfull of humans, high prey drive, high energy, destructive, extremely independent and being escape artists.
